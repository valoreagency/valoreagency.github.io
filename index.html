Common Questions

Questions we hear often.

What is fractional brand management?

Fractional brand management means bringing in a senior brand strategist part-time rather than hiring a full-time employee. You get experienced brand leadership at a fraction of the cost, without the hiring risk or lengthy onboarding process.

How is Valore different from a marketing agency?

Valore does not run campaigns, post to social media, or manage ad accounts. Valore provides strategic brand leadership: clarity on your positioning, a complete 12-month roadmap, and oversight of the vendors inside your business. The scope covers identity, voice, client experience, and market presence, not just marketing channels.

Who is the right fit for Valore?

Valore works best with established, owner-led businesses that have grown through reputation and referrals but need a more intentional brand presence to reach the next level. Common clients include renovation companies, interior designers, coaches, consultants, specialty trades, and premium professional firms at the high six or seven-figure stage.

How much does fractional brand management cost?

Every engagement begins with the Brand Intelligence Sprint at $4,500 CAD. This produces your complete 12-month roadmap in five days. The Brand Oversight Retainer is $3,500 CAD per month for ongoing fractional brand leadership, vendor accountability, and roadmap execution.

What happens on the discovery call?

Derek reviews your business, website, reviews, and competitive landscape before the call. The 30 minutes is spent identifying the specific gap between where your brand is and where it needs to be. You walk away with at least one clear direction, regardless of whether you move forward with Valore.
