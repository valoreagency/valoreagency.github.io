Common Questions

Questions we hear often.

What is fractional brand management?

Fractional brand management means bringing in a senior brand strategist part-time rather than hiring a full-time employee. You get experienced brand leadership at a fraction of the cost, without the hiring risk or lengthy onboarding process.

How is Valore different from a marketing agency?

Valore does not run campaigns, post to social media, or manage ad accounts. We provide strategic brand leadership: clarity on your positioning, a clear growth roadmap, and oversight of the vendors and channels already inside your business. Think fractional executive, not agency.

Who is the right fit for Valore?

Valore works best with premium service businesses that have grown through referrals and reputation but need a more intentional brand presence to reach the next level. Common clients include high-end renovation companies, interior designers, business coaches, and specialty trades generating over $200,000 annually.

How much does fractional brand management cost?

Pricing is determined after a discovery call based on the right package for your business. As a guide, Valore is approximately half the cost of hiring a full-time marketing manager, with no long-term hiring commitment.

What happens on the discovery call?

Derek reviews your business, website, reviews, and competitive landscape before the call. The 30 minutes is spent identifying the specific gap between where your brand is and where it needs to be. You walk away with at least one clear direction, regardless of whether you move forward with Valore.
