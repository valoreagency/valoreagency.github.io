01

Brand Intelligence Sprint

For established, owner-led businesses that want to know exactly where their brand stands, what is holding it back, and what to do about it in the right order. This is where every Valore engagement begins. The Sprint is not a discovery exercise. It is a deliverable.

What's Included
  • Full digital audit: website, socials, GBP, SEO, AI visibility, reputation, competitive landscape
  • Brand Intelligence Session: a structured, in-depth conversation covering the business, brand, identity, client experience, and goals
  • Strategic synthesis across six brand dimensions: Identity, Voice, Experience, Visibility, Conversion, and Systems
  • Complete 12-month roadmap deployed live to ClickUp
  • Every task assigned by owner, priority, effort, impact, and category
  • Vendor recommendations with rationale and estimated costs
  • Strategic framework analysis across four proven methodologies
What This Addresses
  • No clear picture of where the brand actually stands
  • Marketing investment with no strategic framework behind it
  • Inconsistent brand across identity, voice, and client experience
  • No roadmap — reacting to opportunities instead of executing a plan
  • Not knowing which vendors to hire or what to tell them
  • A brand that does not reflect the quality of the business behind it
The Outcome

A complete 12-month strategic roadmap in a live ClickUp workspace. Every task has an owner, a priority, and a concrete checklist. You know exactly what to do, in what order, and who should do it. The roadmap covers every surface of your brand, not just the marketing.

Investment

$4,500. One-time. Delivered within five business days of your Brand Intelligence Session.

02

Brand Oversight Retainer

For clients who want Valore to stay in the room after the Sprint. The roadmap is built. The retainer keeps it moving. Valore stays involved as your fractional brand director, not as a vendor executing tasks. That is what the vendors on your roadmap are for.

What's Included
  • Monthly 60-minute strategy session
  • Weekly ClickUp review, task approvals, and course corrections
  • Vendor sourcing, briefing, and ongoing accountability
  • Priority async access between sessions for time-sensitive decisions
  • Quarterly roadmap recalibration as priorities and the market shift
  • Honest reads on what is working and what is not
What This Addresses
  • Roadmaps that stall because no one is accountable to them
  • Vendors doing their own thing with no alignment to the brand
  • Owner still making every brand decision alone
  • No senior perspective when major decisions come up
  • Drift between the strategy and what is actually being executed
  • Growth that still requires the principal to push it forward
What This Is Not

The retainer does not include copywriting, design, video editing, ad management, or any hands-on production work. Valore is your brand director. The vendors on your roadmap handle the execution. Valore manages the relationship.

Investment

$3,500 per month. Follows completion of the Brand Intelligence Sprint.
