How It Works

A clear process.
No guessing about what comes next.

Every engagement starts the same way: with Derek doing the work before you ever get on a call. Here is what happens from there.

01

Book a Discovery Call

The first step is a 30-minute call. Before you get on, Derek reviews your business: website, social presence, reviews, competitive landscape, and anything else relevant to your market. By the time you talk, he already knows your world.

The call is not a pitch. It is a focused session to identify the gap between where your brand is and where it needs to be. You walk away with at least one clear direction, regardless of whether you move forward with Valore.

30 minutes. No cost. No obligation.
02

Brand Assessment

If there is a clear fit, Derek conducts a deeper brand assessment. This is a thorough look at your positioning, messaging, current marketing channels, vendor relationships, digital presence, and how you compare to the competition.

The goal is to understand not just what you are doing, but what is missing and what needs to change for the brand to start reflecting the quality of the business behind it.

03

The Roadmap

Based on the assessment, Derek builds a clear growth roadmap specific to your business. This is not a generic marketing plan. It is a prioritized set of recommendations: what to build, what to stop, and in what order.

You know exactly what to focus on and why. Nothing is vague. Nothing is left open to interpretation.

04

Implementation and Oversight

Depending on the package, Derek either delivers the roadmap and supports from a strategic distance, or stays involved as your ongoing fractional brand leader. That means overseeing vendors, holding people accountable, and adjusting the strategy as the business grows.

He is not running your ads or posting your content. He is making sure the people who do are doing it in the right direction, for the right reasons, and getting the right results.

05

Regular Check-ins and Refinement

For ongoing clients, Derek holds regular check-in sessions to review progress, adjust the roadmap, and make sure everything stays aligned to the brand direction.

Nothing drifts. Nothing goes unreviewed. Markets shift, priorities change, and vendors come and go. The roadmap evolves with the business.

This is what it means to have a fractional brand leader, not just a one-time consultant.

What You Actually Get

Senior brand leadership from day one.
