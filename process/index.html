How It Works

Five days to a roadmap.
Then someone accountable to it.

Every Valore engagement starts the same way: a focused sprint that produces a complete 12-month strategy for your brand. Not a marketing plan. A roadmap for every surface your business shows up on, built to move you from standard, to premium, and toward THE BRAND in your market.

01

Discovery Call

A 30-minute call. Before you get on, your business is reviewed: website, social presence, reviews, competitive landscape, and anything else relevant to your market. By the time you connect, the picture is already forming.

This is not a pitch. It is a focused conversation to determine whether there is a real fit and what the engagement looks like. You walk away with at least one clear direction, regardless of whether you move forward.

30 minutes. No cost. No obligation.
02

The Research Audit

Before the Brand Intelligence Session, your business is audited across every digital surface: website messaging and conversion, social presence and consistency, Google Business Profile, how you appear in AI search results, SEO performance, online reputation, and the competitive landscape in your market.

This is the intelligence layer. It reveals what the data shows about your brand before you say a word. By the time the session begins, the research is done and the gaps are already mapped.

Completed before your session. You do nothing.
03

Brand Intelligence Session

A structured, in-depth conversation that goes well beyond marketing. It covers the business model, how the brand describes itself, visual identity and consistency, client onboarding and experience, what clients receive and how it is delivered, marketing channels, goals, and what is actually standing in the way.

Every answer feeds directly into the strategic analysis. This is not a discovery call. It is the source material for your roadmap. The questions are sequenced to surface what a standard agency intake would never find.

60 to 90 minutes. Conducted on a call.
04

Roadmap Build and Delivery

Research and session intelligence are synthesized into a complete 12-month strategic roadmap specific to your business. Not a template with your name on it. A prioritized plan built from everything your brand actually shows, says, and delivers.

The roadmap covers six dimensions of the brand: Identity, Voice, Experience, Visibility, Conversion, and Systems. Every task is assigned to an owner, tagged by priority, effort, and impact, and comes with a concrete action checklist. Vendor recommendations are included with rationale and estimated costs.

The roadmap is not a PDF. It is deployed live to ClickUp, ready to work from on day one.

Delivered within five business days of the session.
05

Brand Oversight Retainer

For clients who want Valore to stay in the room. The Sprint produces the roadmap. The retainer keeps it moving.

Each month: a 60-minute strategy session, weekly ClickUp review and approvals, vendor sourcing and accountability, and course corrections as priorities shift. Valore stays involved as your fractional brand director, not as a vendor doing the production work. That is what the vendors are for.

Quarterly, the roadmap is recalibrated. What has moved gets marked complete. What has shifted gets reprioritized. The strategy stays current as the business grows.

This is what it means to have a brand director, not just a one-time consultant.
